What is the game:

Counter Strike: Global Offensive (CS: GO for short) is a cult classic Multiplayer First-Person Shooter developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. It is the 4th iteration in the Counter Strike series and the latest. Originally the Counter Strike game was a mod game mode for the Half-Life series before the rights for the intellectual property were acquired by Valve. The game is about Terrorists who are on the attacking side try to commit an act of terror (planting the bomb) on either one of the 2 sites on the map (A site, and B site). The counter terrorists are the Defenders whose roles are to either defend the bomb sites and prevent the terrorists from planting the bomb or defusing the bomb, there are 30 rounds total in the game that can be plated, whoever wins 16 rounds first wins the game, if the teams tie 15-15 the game goes into overtime. If either side kills the opposing side completely, they also win the round. Each team switches sides at the second half of the game, terrorists become counter-terrorists and vice-versa.

Changed the way competitive shooters are played today and define the layout of competitive shooter formula. Each round is strictly timed, with no respawning when you die, the only time a player can respawn is in the new round. Raises the skill ceiling of the game, every action you make has consequences, the typical running and gunning does not work in comparison to other shooters like the big-name ones such as Call of duty, Halo, Fortnite etc.

The round-based combat system, with clear goals, minimal randomness, no respawns and definitive winners and losers each round, made Counterstrike a great choice for high-level competitive play. First tournament was hosted in 2000, and it became a major international title in 2001. A new version was released with source engine in 2004, players embraced it but also kept playing to original 1.6 version. 2012 Is when CSGO bought in the second wave of dominance over the competitive E-sports scene.
