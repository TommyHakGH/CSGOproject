The way it works: CSGO introduced skins for guns and knives to make the game more fun and allow for more community involvement. These skins are put into a loot crate and the nicer the skin the lower the chance to get it, with knife skins being the rarest. The skins also come in different status grade ranging from battle scarred to factory new, as the skin nears battle scar you can see less of the design on the gun and see more wear and tear on it, compared to factory new where the skin would be in pristine condition.
